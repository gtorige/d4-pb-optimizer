Diablo 4 Paragon Optimizer

Paragon library

Bundled data from DiabloTools/d4data — datamined for the current Lord of Hatred / Season 13 build. Includes all 8 classes: Barbarian, Druid, Necromancer, Sorcerer, Rogue, Paladin, Spiritborn, Warlock. Board names are placeholders (Start / Board 1..N); use the mini-grid layout to identify boards. You can swap in a different dataset with Load dataset….

Pick a class, add up to 5 boards to your chain and pick glyphs. Each chain slot has its own rotation, Magic / Rare / Legendary filters, a pinned glyph, and a node-picker. Nodes you explicitly check in Pick nodes are required — the optimizer must route through them and prefers the shortest connecting path.

          Stats accumulated by activated nodes (and glyph bonuses) are summed per bucket. Final score = base × product of (1 + bucket_total) over all buckets. Use weights to scale stat contributions.
